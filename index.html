Quarantine Chef

While I've been stuck in our house, cooking and baking have become my outlet to channel all my fears and anxieties. We have to cook what is in the refrigerator or what is in the kitchen to continue life. I can't deny my hunger every hour so I started watching on youtube the foods I was looking for, everytime I get hungry youtube is the one I approached to watch pinoy recipes and try to get full. Over the course of the day, my cooking became even more honed even though I'm no longer watch youtube. Also, no wasted vegetables and fish at home because I make it delicious dish.
